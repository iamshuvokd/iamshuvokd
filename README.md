## Executive Profile

I am a **Senior Software Developer** specializing in business applications, workflow automation, reporting systems, and full-stack product development. I combine practical software engineering with Microsoft Power Platform expertise to build solutions that are reliable, maintainable, and aligned with business operations.

My work focuses on turning complex processes into clean digital systems: internal applications, approval workflows, dashboards, integrations, data-driven tools, and modern web applications that teams can use with confidence.
